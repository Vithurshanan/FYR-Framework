# Energy-Efficient Container Consolidation Framework
## Research Questions and Objectives

## Research Focus

The research focuses exclusively on the **Energy-Efficient Container Consolidation Framework** for sustainable software deployment. This work does not include SDN (Software-Defined Networking) or Blockchain components. These technologies may be considered for future enhancements but are not part of the current research objectives.

---

## Research Questions

### RQ1: Energy Efficiency
**How can container consolidation strategies reduce energy consumption in cloud data centers while maintaining service quality?**

This research question investigates:
- The effectiveness of container consolidation algorithms in reducing energy consumption
- The trade-offs between energy savings and service quality (SLA compliance)
- The impact of consolidation strategies on overall system performance
- Optimal consolidation thresholds and parameters

### RQ2: Resource Utilization
**What is the optimal balance between resource utilization and energy consumption in containerized cloud environments?**

This research question explores:
- The relationship between CPU/memory utilization and energy consumption
- Optimal resource utilization levels for energy efficiency
- The impact of dynamic resource allocation on energy savings
- Multi-tier consolidation strategies

### RQ3: Performance Impact
**How do energy-efficient consolidation strategies affect system performance metrics such as latency and throughput?**

This research question examines:
- The correlation between consolidation decisions and performance metrics
- The impact of container migration on latency and throughput
- Performance trade-offs in energy-efficient scheduling
- SLA compliance under energy-optimized configurations

### RQ4: Environmental Impact
**What is the environmental impact (carbon footprint) of energy-efficient container consolidation in cloud data centers?**

This research question assesses:
- Carbon footprint reduction through energy-efficient consolidation
- Cost-benefit analysis of energy optimization strategies
- Environmental impact metrics and reporting
- Sustainability indicators and KPIs

### RQ5: Scalability and Adaptability
**How scalable and adaptable are energy-efficient container consolidation frameworks for different workload patterns and infrastructure configurations?**

This research question evaluates:
- Framework scalability with varying numbers of hosts and containers
- Adaptability to different workload patterns (bursty, steady-state, periodic)
- Performance under different infrastructure configurations
- Real-time monitoring and adaptation capabilities

## Research Objectives

### Objective 1: Design and Implement Energy-Efficient Container Consolidation Framework
**Mapping to Research Questions:** RQ1, RQ2, RQ5

**Specific Goals:**
- Design a multi-layer architecture for energy-efficient container consolidation
- Implement consolidation algorithms that optimize energy consumption
- Develop dynamic resource pooling and scheduling mechanisms
- Create workload orchestration layer with energy-aware scheduling
- Implement real-time monitoring and adaptation capabilities

**Success Criteria:**
- Framework successfully reduces energy consumption by at least 20%
- Framework maintains SLA compliance (response time, availability)
- Framework scales to 100+ containers across 10+ hosts
- Framework adapts to changing workload patterns in real-time

### Objective 2: Develop Energy Monitoring and Sustainability Management System
**Mapping to Research Questions:** RQ4

**Specific Goals:**
- Implement real-time energy consumption tracking
- Develop carbon footprint calculation and reporting
- Create environmental impact assessment tools
- Design cost estimation and analysis features
- Build comprehensive KPI generation and reporting system

**Success Criteria:**
- Real-time energy monitoring with <2 second update interval
- Accurate carbon footprint calculation (within 5% of actual)
- Comprehensive environmental impact reports
- Cost estimation accuracy within 10%
- Automated KPI generation and reporting

### Objective 3: Evaluate Performance Impact of Energy-Efficient Consolidation
**Mapping to Research Questions:** RQ3

**Specific Goals:**
- Measure latency and throughput under consolidation strategies
- Analyze performance trade-offs in energy-optimized configurations
- Evaluate SLA compliance under energy-efficient scheduling
- Assess container migration impact on performance metrics
- Develop performance optimization strategies

**Success Criteria:**
- Latency increase <10% compared to non-optimized configurations
- Throughput maintained at >90% of baseline
- SLA compliance rate >95%
- Container migration overhead <5% of total processing time
- Performance metrics documented and analyzed
